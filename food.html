Indian Food

Biryani

A flavorful rice dish made with spices, saffron, and marinated meat. Famous in Hyderabad and Lucknow.

Dosa

A crispy South Indian dish made from fermented rice batter. Often served with chutney and sambar.

Pani Puri

A popular street food with crispy puris filled with spicy water, potatoes, and chutney.
